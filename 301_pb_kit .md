These platforms are designed for complex projects in AIoT, machine vision, and edge computing, offering high performance and extensive connectivity options.

## What Are PBKit 301‑A and PBKit 301‑A‑Base?


**PBKit 301‑A** is a powerful mainboard based on the Rockchip RK3568 processor. It’s ideal for AI‑enabled projects, machine vision, and industrial applications.

**PBKit 301‑A‑Base** is a complete development platform that includes PBKit 301‑A plus a base board. It provides additional interfaces, power management, and expansion options for building robust systems.

## Key Features


### PBKit 301‑A

- **Processor**: RK3568 — 64‑bit quad‑core AIoT processor based on Cortex‑A55 with an integrated NPU (1 TOPS) and GPU Mali‑G52 (800 MHz, 38.4 GFLOPS).
- **Memory**: 4 GB LPDDR4 RAM, 16 GB eMMC storage.
- **Interfaces**:
- 2×USB 3.0, 2×PCIe 3.0, 3×SATA 3.0;
- 2×Ethernet (1 Gbps), 3×CAN‑FD, 10×UART;
- Display interfaces: HDMI (4K), LVDS, MIPI‑DSI, RGB, eDP (support for synchronous/asynchronous output on 3 screens);
- Built‑in ISP: 12 Mb/s, 1920×1080@60 fps, 2×MIPI‑CSI inputs.
- **Operating Systems**: Linux 4.19, Android 11, Debian, Ubuntu 20.04.
- **Power**: 12 V input, included adapter.


### PBKit 301‑A‑Base


- Includes PBKit 301‑A and a base board with additional features;
- Expanded I/O capabilities and power management;
- Pre‑installed Linux OS, ready for development.
